    Health Talks

    1
    Proper HangingHang the net over the sleeping area every evening. Ensure it is tucked securely under the mattress or mat on all sides.
    2
    Consistent UsageSleep under the ITN every night, even during short afternoon naps. Mosquitoes bite mostly between dusk and dawn.
    3
    Protect Vulnerable GroupsEnsure all children (especially under 5 years) and pregnant women sleep under ITNs every night.
    4
    Washing & CareWash gently with mild soap and cold water. Hang to dry in the shade, never in direct sunlight.
    5
    Important Don'tsDo not use the net for fishing, fencing, or gardening. Do not store the net when it is wet.
    6
    ReplacementReplace the ITN every 3 years, or sooner if it develops large holes.
