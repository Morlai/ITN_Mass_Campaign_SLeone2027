Health Talks

1
Proper Hanging Hang the net over the sleeping area every evening. Ensure it is tucked securely under the mattress or mat on all sides.
2
Consistent Usage Sleep under the ITN every night, even during short afternoon naps. Mosquitoes bite mostly between dusk and dawn.
3
Protect Vulnerable Groups Ensure all children (especially under 5 years) and pregnant women sleep under ITNs every night.
4
Washing & Care Wash gently with mild soap and cold water. Hang to dry in the shade, never in direct sunlight. Limit washing to 3 times per year.
5
Important Don'ts Do not use the net for fishing, fencing, or gardening. Do not store the net when it is wet.
6
Replacement Replace the ITN every 3 years, or sooner if it develops large holes. Report damaged nets to community health workers.
